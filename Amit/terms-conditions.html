C. Description Of The Game

Both formats of the Game begin with the User being allotted a certain amount of fictitious money by the FTL app to spend during the Game. The same amount of this fictitious money shall be made available to each participating User in a single Game. As highlighted in Section A above, in the Practice Nets format, the User is not required to purchase Coins while in the Play-off League, Coins will have to be purchased by the User from the FTL app and redeemed accordingly to play each single round of the Play-off League. The User can win prizes for good performance only in the Play-off League. No prizes are awarded to the User in the Practice Nets. However, a leader-board depicting the rank of the User amongst the participating Users in that Game will be calculated by the FTL app in both formats.
In the Play-off League format the User, redeeming his/her required number of Coins as earlier purchased on the FTL app will join the Play-off League via the home page of the app. The User is then required to create a portfolio of the Assets on offer on the FTL app, which correspond to real assets, forex, commodities or crypto currencies listed in the relevant markets. The User must purchase a minimum number of units of these Assets as required by the Game with the fictitious cash, in order to be considered as a valid participant in the Game.
The Game in both formats will begin at the designated time indicated on the home page for both the formats. The start and end time of the Game shall be decided by TTPL at its own discretion.
Once the Game in either format begins, The FTL app will track the upward or downward movement of these Assets on the relevant markets without limitation on that particular day, hour, week, month, fortnight or bi-annually. Depending on the portfolios created by each User, the FTL app will, at the closure of that League, calculate the average percentage increase or decrease in the overall value of the portfolios created by the Users, which shall be notionally based on the actual performance of such Assets on the relevant market. The calculation and results shall be based on the delayed data received by TTPL from the markets through its third party vendor. Hence, the results on the basis of which the overall ranking of the Users will be determined, shall not be real-time data but a delayed transmission of the publicly available data received from the markets.
The Users depending on their percentage increase or decrease in their Asset portfolio on the app shall be ranked accordingly from the highest to the lowest amongst all the registered Users participating in either format. The participating User whose fictitious portfolio shall record the maximum percentage increase by the end of the League, shall win the Game. The top ranked Users by the end of the Game in the Play-off, shall be eligible to receive cash prizes of varying values as decided on the FTL app depending upon the total number of participants and the prize pool accumulated for that Game. The Users shall be informed of the value of the prizes on the FTL app prior to joining the Play-off League with the prize amount earning of the User decreasing accordingly with a lower rank. The number of participating Users eligible to obtain a prize amount in the Game shall be decided by TTPL prior to the start of the Game and participating Users shall be able to view the same before the Game begins.
The winning Users will be eligible to withdraw their winnings from the FTL app either directly into their bank accounts as earlier registered with FTL’s payment service provider or use the entire winnings or any part thereof to purchase further Coins on the FTL app in order to redeem them to join future Play-off Leagues on the app.  The total amount of prize money for the Play-off Game, will depend upon the total revenue collected via FTL through the purchase of minimum required number of Coins by all the participating Users entering that Game. From the total amount collected, a major portion of that amount will be utilised towards forming the prize pool of the Play-off League after deducting necessary taxes. Therefore, the size of the overall prize pool for each Play-off League depends automatically on the total number of Users opting to participate in a particular Play-off League and is subject to fluctuate. TTPL has no direct role in fixing the prize amount for any Game on the FTL app. The Users are informed that there are two primary forms of taxes that would be applicable to the payments made through or received from the FTL app i.e. (i) GST and (ii) TDS. As explained in section ‘Q’ below, while purchasing the Coins on the FTL app, the Users will pay GST in addition to the value of the Coins purchased in each transaction, while during the withdrawal of prize money, TDS as applicable will be deducted from the winnings before the same is transferred into the selected bank account of the User.

Market Page:

Once on this page, the User will be provided with 3 (three) segregations of performance categories of the Assets which were provided on the FTL app for selection by the Users:
‍
Top Gainers: This category displays all those Assets in descending order, the percentage value of which has increased the maximum on the relevant market in which it is being traded, since the start of the Game.

Through this page, the User can keep track of the upward or downward movement of his/her favourite/ preferred set of Assets on the relevant market at any point of time during the Game. The Game in the Practice Nets format shall end at the time decided by TTPL and/or the FTL admin team.

Selection of the winning Users
‍
